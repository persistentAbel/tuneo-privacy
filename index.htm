Privacy Policy for Tuneo – AI Music Generator

Last Updated: November 2025
Developer: Arturo Gomez
Contact: iosabeldev@gmail.com

Tuneo (“the App”) is an AI-powered tool that allows users to generate music and lyrics. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Tuneo does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, emails, phone numbers
  • Account or login information
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, videos, or device files
  • Advertising identifiers or analytics data
  • Any sensitive personal information

2. AI Content Processing

When you enter text or lyrics to generate music, your input is sent to third-party service providers solely to process your request and produce the AI-generated content. These providers do not receive personal identifying information. We do not store your input or use it to train AI models.

3. Local Storage (Saved Songs)

Generated music files are saved locally on your device. They are not uploaded or shared. You may delete this data at any time by removing the App from your device or clearing its storage through your device settings.

4. Purchases & Subscriptions

The App uses a third-party service to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. This service processes anonymous purchase receipts to verify your active subscription. No personal information is collected, stored, or associated with these receipts.

5. No Ads and No Tracking

Tuneo does not display any third-party advertising, does not use tracking technologies, and does not track users across apps or websites.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App does not collect personal data and is suitable for users of all ages. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that personal information may have been submitted, please contact us.

7. Security

All communication with third-party processing services is transmitted over secure, encrypted connections (HTTPS).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to the App. The “Last Updated” date will be revised whenever changes are made.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: iosabeldev@gmail.com
