Privacy Policy for Tuneo – AI Music Generator

Last Updated: November 2025
Developer: Arturo Gomez
Contact: iosabeldev@gmail.com

Tuneo (“the App”) is an AI-powered music and lyrics generation tool that allows users to create songs using artificial intelligence. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Tuneo does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Specifically, we do NOT collect: name, email address, phone number, account information, location data, contact list, photos, videos, device files, sensitive personal data, advertising identifiers, analytics, or device identifiers.

2. AI-Generated Content (Lyrics + Music Processing)

When users submit lyrics or text for music generation, input is processed securely through our endpoint on Vercel and forwarded to Replicate or other AI models only to fulfill the user’s request. Tuneo does not store or log user content and does not use it for training.

3. Local Storage (Songs Saved on Device)

Generated music files are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to any server. You can remove this data at any time by deleting the App from your device or via your device’s storage settings.

4. Subscriptions & Purchases

Tuneo uses RevenueCat to manage subscriptions, which processes anonymized purchase receipts and subscription status. RevenueCat does not receive personal identity information or your generated content.

5. No Third-Party Advertising and No Tracking

The App does not display ads, use tracking technologies, or share data with advertisers. We do not track users across apps or websites.

6. Children’s Privacy

Tuneo does not collect personal data and is suitable for users of all ages.

7. Security

All communication with third-party AI and payment services uses encrypted HTTPS.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes to the App. The “Last Updated” date will be revised accordingly.

9. Contact Us
